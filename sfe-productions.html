Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Visual Excellence

Sound Factory Entertainment was founded by Bezariel Mwanza with a singular vision — to bring world-class audio and visual production to Zambia. Based in the heart of Lusaka, we've spent over a decade perfecting the art of storytelling through the lens.

From intimate wedding ceremonies to large-scale corporate events, conferences, and music productions — our team brings unmatched creativity, technical precision, and an eye for the moments that matter most.
